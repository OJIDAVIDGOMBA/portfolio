The Process
Behind Every Cut.

01
Brief & Align

We align on your brand voice, content goals, platform, and turnaround needs. I study your existing content so every clip feels native to you.

→
02

Drop your raw footage or recording into our shared space. No need to log clips or write notes — I handle the full review and selection.

→
03

04
